# Product Principles

These principles govern every engineering decision.

## 1. Ledger First

Financial data is immutable.

Balances are derived from transactions.

Never edit history.

---

## 2. Workspace First

Users work inside contextual workspaces.

Avoid giant CRUD tables.

---

## 3. Mobile First

Every workflow must be usable on a phone.

---

## 4. Audit Everything

Every important action is recorded.

Nothing disappears.

---

## 5. Progressive Complexity

Simple workflows remain simple.

Advanced functionality appears only when needed.

---

## 6. Human Friendly

The software should speak the language of transport businesses.

Example:

"Khata"

instead of

"Receivable Ledger"

where appropriate.

---

## 7. Modular Architecture

Every module should be independently maintainable.

---

## 8. No Duplicate Data

One source of truth.

Derived values instead of duplicated fields.

---

## 9. Event Based Finance

Everything that affects money becomes a financial event.

---

## 10. Future Ready

Architecture must support SaaS expansion without redesign.
